# 📊 Financial KPI Dashboard

## 🔍 Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to identify profitability trends and business insights.

# 📊 Financial KPI Dashboard

## 🔍 Business Problem

Retail businesses often struggle to identify loss-making transactions due to aggressive discounting strategies and lack of visibility into profit margins. This results in hidden losses and inefficient decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze historical sales data and build an interactive dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor key financial KPIs, identify loss-making areas, and make data-driven pricing decisions.

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Identified **18.7% of transactions were loss-making**, primarily driven by high discount rates
* High-discount orders showed **average profit margins of -32.4%**, indicating unsustainable pricing strategies
* Certain regions and product categories consistently underperformed in profitability
* Seasonal trends revealed fluctuations in revenue and profit across years

---

## 🚀 Business Impact

* Helps businesses **reduce losses by optimizing discount strategies**
* Enables **data-driven decision-making** through clear visibility into KPIs
* Supports identification of **high-risk segments and underperforming categories**
* Improves overall profitability by highlighting actionable insights
